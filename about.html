Real Madrid Facts

Real Madrid fans name their club as madridista where the number of a fan base reaches over 228 million supporters exist around the world
Real Madrid actually had been named as the ‘Best Football Club of the 20th Century’ by FIFA.
Real Madrid have won a record 21 trophies; a record 14 European Cup/UEFA Champions League titles, two UEFA Cups, and a record five UEFA Super Cups. In worldwide competitions, they have achieved a record seven club world championships.
